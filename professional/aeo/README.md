# AEO — AI Engine Optimization

## Overview
Content strategy and production pipeline to build authority in AI-driven search results (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, etc.) for Wayground's key markets.

---

## Pipeline Stages

```
1. KW Identification
   ↓
2. Relevancy & Winning Potential Check
   ↓
3. Content Strategy Design
   ↓
4. Content Generation / Writing
   ↓
5. AEO Readiness Check
   ↓
6. Teacher / SME Vetting
   ↓
7. Page Design
   ↓
8. Production
```

### Stage Details

| # | Stage | Folder | Description |
|---|-------|--------|-------------|
| 1 | KW Identification | `keywords/` | Keyword research and discovery |
| 2 | Relevancy & Winning Potential | `keywords/` | Assess relevancy to Wayground + feasibility of ranking |
| 3 | Content Strategy | `strategy/` | Define content format, structure, angle |
| 4 | Content Generation | `content/` | Draft content (AI-assisted or manual) |
| 5 | AEO Readiness Check | `content/` | Verify content meets AEO criteria |
| 6 | Teacher/SME Vetting | `vetting/` | Subject matter expert review and approval |
| 7 | Page Design | `production/` | Visual design and layout |
| 8 | Production | `production/` | Final assets, deployment-ready |
