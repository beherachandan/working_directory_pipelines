# EAR Map: What Is Differentiation in Teaching? A Complete Guide for Educators

**Primary Entity:** Differentiated Instruction
**Canonical Definition:** Differentiated instruction is a teaching approach in which educators adjust their methods, content, process, and assessment to meet the diverse learning needs, readiness levels, and interests of individual students.
