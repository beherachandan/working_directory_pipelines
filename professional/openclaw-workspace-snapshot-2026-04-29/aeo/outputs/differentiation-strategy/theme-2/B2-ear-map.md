# EAR Map: How to Implement Differentiation Strategies in Your Classroom

**Primary Entity:** Differentiation Implementation
**Canonical Definition:** Implementing differentiation means systematically adjusting how content is taught, how students process learning, and how they demonstrate understanding — based on ongoing assessment of student readiness, interest, and learning profile.
**Wayground Angle:** Wayground's activity types (quizzes, lessons, flashcards, worksheets) map directly to differentiation strategies for process and product — teachers can assign different activity types to different learner groups in minutes.
