# Objection Handling

Top objections with persona-specific responses. Based on win/loss analysis: 1,030 won vs 3,223 lost deals.

**Loss patterns informing responses:**
- #1 loss: Cold/unresponsive (843 deals) — never engaged at all
- #2 loss: No budget (588 deals) — timing/funding issue
- Competitor losses: IXL (math practice), MagicSchool (AI tools)

---

## "We already use Kahoot / Nearpod / Gimkit"

**Core response:** Wayground does what they do PLUS formative assessment with real data.

| Persona | Response |
|---------|----------|
| **Superintendent** | "Kahoot/Nearpod is great for engagement, but it doesn't give you district-wide assessment data or Common Assessment capabilities. Many districts keep their engagement tool AND add Wayground for the assessment layer — or consolidate and save." |
| **Curriculum Director** | "The question is whether your current tool gives you the formative assessment data you need. Can teachers create standards-aligned assessments? Do you get cross-building data? Can you run Common Assessments? Those are the gaps we fill." |
| **Principal** | "Your teachers probably love [tool] — and they can keep using it. But Wayground adds the assessment and data piece that gives YOU visibility into what's happening in classrooms." |
| **Teacher Champion** | "Totally — [tool] is fun. Wayground gives you the fun AND tells you which students get it and which don't. You get the engagement plus the data. Most teachers use both initially, then consolidate." |

## "No budget this year"

**Core response:** Timing play — plant seeds now, align to budget cycle.

| Persona | Response |
|---------|----------|
| **Superintendent** | "When does your budget cycle start for next year? I'd love to get this in front of you early so it's included in planning. In the meantime, your [X] teachers can keep using the free version — we'll make sure they're set up for success so you have usage data for your budget justification." |
| **Curriculum Director** | "Understood. Can we set up a pilot at no cost so you have data for your next budget cycle? That way you'll have teacher feedback AND usage metrics when it's time to request funding." |
| **Principal** | "I hear you on budget. The good news is your teachers are already using the free version — that costs you nothing right now. When budget opens up, you'll have real usage data to justify the investment." |
| **Teacher Champion** | "No worries — keep using the free version and building your case. I can help you put together a usage summary to share with your admin when budget conversations start. [X] games and [Y] students is a compelling story." |

## "We need to see student growth data"

**Core response:** Acknowledge the gap honestly, pivot to what we DO provide.

| Persona | Response |
|---------|----------|
| **Superintendent** | "Longitudinal student growth tracking is something we're investing in. Today, Wayground gives you real-time formative data — question-level performance, participation trends, and assessment results across your district. For summative growth, most districts pair us with their existing assessment platform." |
| **Curriculum Director** | "Fair point — we're strong on formative assessment data: item analysis, class performance, cross-building comparisons. For multi-year growth trajectories, we complement rather than replace your summative tools. The formative data fills the gap between benchmark windows." |

**Honest caveat:** This is a known competitive gap. IXL is stronger on longitudinal student-level growth. Don't oversell here.

## "Free version is enough"

**Core response:** It's enough until it isn't — and their usage data shows where it isn't.

| Persona | Response |
|---------|----------|
| **Superintendent** | "The free version is great for individual teachers. But you don't get district visibility, Common Assessment, or admin reporting. The question is: do you want to see what your [X] teachers are doing, or just know they're doing something?" |
| **Curriculum Director** | "For individual classroom use, yes. But Common Assessment, cross-building data, and accommodation features are premium. If you're trying to build a coordinated assessment strategy, free won't get you there." |
| **Teacher Champion** | "I hear that a lot! But you've hit [X] paywall limits this month — specifically [features]. Those features exist because teachers like you need them. Let me show you what you're missing." |

## "We're locked into another platform"

**Core response:** Coexistence first, consolidation later.

| Persona | Response |
|---------|----------|
| **All** | "We don't need to replace anything today. Most districts start by using Wayground alongside their current tools — teachers naturally gravitate to what works best. When your current contract comes up, you'll have usage data to make an informed decision. When does your current agreement end?" |

## "Need IT approval / security review"

**Core response:** Be prepared and proactive — don't let this stall the deal.

| Persona | Response |
|---------|----------|
| **All** | "Absolutely — we take data privacy seriously. I'll send over our security documentation, SOC 2 report, data processing agreement, and FERPA/COPPA compliance details. Can you connect me with your IT/security team directly? I'd rather get ahead of this than let it become a bottleneck." |

**Action items:**
- Provide security documentation proactively
- Offer direct call with Wayground security team
- Send FERPA/COPPA compliance summary
- Provide data residency and deletion policy details
