# Value Prop: Data Visibility

## One-Liner
"Wayground gives administrators real-time visibility into instructional activity across every classroom, building, and the entire district — without waiting for end-of-quarter reports."

## The Data Proof
Query these BigQuery metrics to back this claim:
- **District-wide usage** — active teachers, games, students by org/district
- **Building comparisons** — school-level adoption and engagement metrics
- **Usage trends** — week-over-week, month-over-month growth trajectories
- **Teacher adoption** — which teachers are active vs. inactive
- **Assessment data** — question-level performance across classrooms
- **Content coverage** — subjects and grade levels with activity

## By Persona

### Superintendent
- Frame: "See what's happening in every classroom, every day — not just at the end of the quarter"
- Lead with: District-wide dashboard, building comparisons, ROI metrics, board-ready reports
- Proof point: "X teachers across Y buildings are generating data — premium gives you the district view"

### Curriculum Director
- Frame: "Know which standards are being assessed, which buildings are aligned, and where gaps exist"
- Lead with: Standards coverage data, cross-building assessment consistency, PLC data sharing
- Proof point: "Track assessment activity across buildings — identify where curriculum fidelity is strong and where it needs support"

### Principal
- Frame: "Building-level data without asking teachers to fill out another spreadsheet"
- Lead with: Building dashboard, teacher activity reports, student engagement metrics
- Proof point: "See which teachers are active, which students are engaged — data flows automatically from classroom use"

### Teacher Champion
- Frame: "Your data works for you — show your admin what you're accomplishing"
- Lead with: Class reports, student progress tracking, shareable data for evaluations
- Proof point: "Your classroom data is already there — premium unlocks detailed reports and exports"

## Competitive Angle
- **vs. Kahoot:** Reports limited to individual game results — no classroom, building, or district aggregation
- **vs. IXL:** Good individual student data but limited teacher/admin visibility and no real-time classroom data
- **vs. Nearpod:** Lesson-level data but no district-wide aggregation
- **vs. Google Classroom:** Activity tracking but no assessment analytics or admin dashboards
- **Wayground advantage:** Real-time data from classroom → building → district in one platform
- **Known gap:** Longitudinal student growth tracking (multi-year) — competitors like IXL stronger here
