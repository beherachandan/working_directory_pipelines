# Wayground Use Case-Specific Messaging
*Source: Cody's PMM/Enablement folder • March 2026*

Comprehensive subject-specific and use-case messaging for teachers and admins.

## Math (Secondary)

### Teacher Value Props
1. **Show the work, not just the answer** — Digital assessments handle algebra to calculus. Students use proper math notation, show step-by-step solutions, get partial credit for process. Graph questions, equation editors, auto-grading that recognizes equivalent answers (0.5 = 1/2 = 50%).
2. **Differentiate without dumbing down** — Same standard, multiple entry points. Struggling students work with smaller numbers, advanced tackle multi-step applications — same assignment. Scaffolding that maintains rigor.
3. **Practice that adapts to each student's needs** — Mastery mode gives struggling students more attempts while pushing advanced students to extensions. No more worksheet packets where half the class is bored and half is lost.

### Admin Value Props
1. **Close the algebra gap before it becomes a crisis** — See exactly which foundational skills students are missing. Track mastery of gateway concepts in real-time. Intervene before algebra becomes the wall that stops college readiness.
2. **Vertical alignment made visible** — Track how 6th-grade ratios build to 7th-grade proportional reasoning to 8th-grade linear relationships. See gaps in vertical progression before they compound.
3. **Math intervention that works** — Identify students needing intervention based on specific skill gaps, not just failing grades. Track intervention effectiveness with precise data. ROI on intervention programs made measurable.

## Math (Elementary)

### Teacher Value Props
1. **Math facts that finally stick** — Turn drill into daily adventures with flashcards. Visual models support every problem — base-10 blocks for place value, arrays for multiplication. Math anxiety melts when practice feels like play.
2. **Word problems without the tears** — Break down complex story problems with step-by-step support. Multiple entry points: struggling students start with pictures, advanced kids jump to equations.
3. **Hands-on math gone digital** — Virtual manipulatives: fraction bars, place value blocks, geometry shapes. Perfect for centers — unlimited supplies.

### Admin Value Props
1. **Foundation skills tracked K-5** — Monitor fact fluency development across grades. Track whether spiral curriculum actually spirals.
2. **Math intervention that compounds success** — Identify students struggling with foundational concepts before they're lost in fractions. Strategic support based on specific skill deficits.
3. **Parent partnership through transparency** — Family-friendly reports showing exactly which math facts need practice at home. School-home alignment that multiplies learning.

## Science (Secondary)

### Teacher Value Props
1. **Lab skills without the lab mess** — Virtual demonstrations, data analysis, hypothesis testing. Perfect for pre-lab prep, absent students, or equipment failures.
2. **Make abstract concepts concrete** — Interactive videos show cellular processes. Drag-and-drop for molecular structures. Simulations for physics principles.
3. **Scientific writing made manageable** — Claim-Evidence-Reasoning prompts built into assessments. Auto-grading helps identify misconceptions.

### Admin Value Props
1. **NGSS alignment without the overwhelm** — Track student progress on disciplinary core ideas, crosscutting concepts, and science practices simultaneously.
2. **Lab safety through digital pre-work** — Students demonstrate understanding before touching equipment. Liability reduction through preparation documentation.
3. **Science achievement gaps addressed systematically** — Target interventions based on actual misconceptions, not general "science struggles."

## Science (Elementary)

### Teacher Value Props
1. **Wonder and inquiry in every lesson** — Virtual field trips: inside a volcano, through the water cycle, into space. Kindergarteners "dissect" flowers; third graders manipulate moon phases.
2. **Science notebooks gone digital** — Draw observations, label diagrams, record data. Voice recordings let pre-writers explain thinking.
3. **STEM integration made simple** — Butterfly unit touches science, reading, writing, and math. Cross-curricular connections that break subject silos.

## Social Studies (Secondary)

3. **Current events connected to curriculum** — Link today's news to historical patterns with interactive lessons. History as living context.

### Admin Value Props
1. **Civic readiness measured and tracked** — Track source analysis, argument evaluation, civic process understanding.
2. **Controversial topics handled professionally** — Balanced resources and structured discussions. Document appropriate handling of sensitive issues.
3. **Interdisciplinary connections made visible** — Track literacy skills developed through content instruction.

## ELA (Secondary)

### Teacher Value Props
1. **Differentiated reading without different books** — Same text, multiple complexity levels. Adjust lexile while maintaining author's voice. One class novel, every student reading.
2. **Writing process made visible** — From brainstorming to final draft. Peer review, revision tracking, targeted feedback. Writing as process, not product.
3. **Grammar in context** — Embed grammar practice within real passages. Skills that transfer to student writing.

### Admin Value Props
1. **Literacy across the curriculum** — Reading comprehension data from every subject, not just ELA.
2. **Writing proficiency at scale** — Track development across grades and genres. Target PD based on actual student writing needs.
3. **Reading intervention that accelerates** — Identify exact reading skill gaps: phonics, fluency, vocabulary, or comprehension.

## Consolidation

### Teacher Value Props
1. **Entire teaching day, streamlined** — Bell ringer, lesson delivery, formative assessment, exit tickets, homework — all in one platform. No more app-switching chaos.
2. **Real-time pivots** — See comprehension gaps during the lesson, not after. Pull small groups while others practice independently.
3. **Classroom management through engagement** — When students are actively responding, behavior problems disappear. Everyone participates.

### Admin Value Props
1. **Instructional consistency without conformity** — Every teacher using common tools while maintaining unique style.
2. **Learning time maximized** — Track time-on-task improvements. ROI through time recovery.
3. **Comprehensive data** — See learning patterns throughout the entire day, not just during assessments.

## Differentiation

### Teacher Value Props
1. **Same destination, different vehicles** — Grade-level standards, personalized paths. Struggling readers get simplified text, advanced get extensions.
2. **Without the paper avalanche** — One digital assignment adapts to each student automatically. No more three versions and color-coded folders.
3. **Dignity preserved** — Students don't know they have different versions. No "low group" worksheets.

### Admin Value Props
1. **Compliance documented automatically** — Every accommodation, modification, support tracked. IEP requirements met through platform features.
2. **Close gaps through appropriate challenge** — Every student in zone of proximal development.
3. **Equity through access, not dilution** — All students access grade-level content with appropriate support.

## MTSS & RTI

### Teacher Value Props
1. **Universal screening that's actually universal** — Assess every student efficiently. Identify Tier 2 needs before students fall further behind. Progress monitoring automated.
2. **Tier 2 interventions without prep overload** — Targeted skill practice auto-adjusts. Small group resources ready to go.
3. **Progress monitoring made painless** — Weekly probes that take minutes. Automatic graphing shows rate of improvement.

### Admin Value Props
1. **MTSS/RTI compliance with complete documentation** — Every screening, intervention, progress monitoring session documented automatically. Legally defensible implementation.
2. **Intervention effectiveness at a glance** — Track which interventions work for which skill deficits. Compare progress rates.
3. **Early warning system** — Catch struggling students early. Reduce inappropriate special education referrals. Prevention cheaper than remediation.

## Multilingual Learners

### Teacher Value Props
1. **Grade-level content made accessible** — Translate directions while keeping academic content in English. L1 support for vocabulary. Access without lowering expectations.
2. **Language scaffolds that fade naturally** — Start with full translation, move to key terms, end with English only.
3. **Speaking confidence through low-stakes practice** — Audio responses let students practice privately. Safe space for language risk-taking.

### Admin Value Props
1. **ESSA compliance through equitable access** — Document how ML students access grade-level content with supports.
2. **Accelerated language acquisition through content** — Both language and academic improvement tracked together.
3. **Family engagement across languages** — Progress reports in home languages.
