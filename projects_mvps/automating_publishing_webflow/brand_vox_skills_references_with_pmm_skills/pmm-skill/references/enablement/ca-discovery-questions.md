# Common Assessment Discovery Questions
*Source: Cody's PMM/Enablement folder • March 2026*

These questions help identify stakeholders, past CA experiences, pain points, and success criteria. NOT intended in order — organized by desired insight.

## 1. Persona: Decision-Making & Purchasing Power
- Who at your district/sites coordinates these assessments?
- Which roles monitor and utilize CA data? What actions do they take after reviewing?
- Who finally approves the purchase?

## 2. Use Case: CA Importance to C&I
- How do you use data to inform curriculum and instruction decisions?
- How are teachers encouraged to differentiate instruction across the district?
- What data do you NOT have that you wish you had at the district level? What would you do with it?
- When, ideally, are CAs delivered? How should data be consumed and leveraged?
- What's the nature of the common assessments in your district?
- How has your CA experience been? (process and outcome, not tool)

## 3. Tools: Pain Points in Replacement Scenario
- What is an example of a successful resource implementation at your district? What were the key steps?
- To what extent is assessment data impacting teaching/learning today?
- Which tools are you currently using for common assessments?
- How has your experience been? (What's working? What's not?)

## 4. Development: Assessment Creation Approaches
- How does your school/district build these assessments?
- Who is involved in development? At what pace do you refresh assessments?
- IF item bank is must-have: What do you require? (standards alignment, DoK, TEI coverage, ability to edit, specific brand, efficacy studies, psychometric validity/reliability)

## 5. Success Criteria: Pilot & Rollout Measurement
- What counts as "good data" when teachers consider lesson planning or pivoting instruction?
- What role do PLCs play in both design and implementation of CAs?
- What specific goals do you want to achieve during a CA pilot or implementation?

## Third-Party Assessment Data Integration
- Which external assessment data do you want to bring in?
- Note: Wayground plans to support CSV uploads (requires student identifiers aligned with Clever/ClassLink records)
- Planned support: State Assessment data for top 10 priority states + Universal Screeners like NWEA MAP Growth
