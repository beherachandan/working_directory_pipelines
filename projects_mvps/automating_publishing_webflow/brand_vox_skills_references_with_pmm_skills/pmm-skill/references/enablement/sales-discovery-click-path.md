*Source: Cody's PMM/Enablement folder • As of 10/30 • March 2026*

## Discovery Based on Topic

### Current Assessment Practices
- What type of common assessments do teachers in your school/district use?
- How often are these assessments administered throughout the year?
- Which platform or tools are used to create, deliver, or analyze the assessments?
- How consistent are assessments across classrooms or schools?

### Teacher Pain Points
- How much time do teachers spend creating or aligning assessments each week?
- What challenges do teachers face in tracking student progress or adjusting instruction?
- How do teachers handle accommodations or differentiation for diverse learners when it comes to assessments?
- Do teachers feel confident that assessments are equitable and curriculum aligned?

### Administrator Needs
- How do you monitor curriculum pacing and standards alignment across classrooms throughout the district?
- What kind of data would help make timely instructional decisions?
- How are assessment results shared with teachers, grade level teams, or leadership?
- Do you have tools to identify gaps or trends across schools, grades, or classrooms?

### Pain Points with Current Tools
- Do your tools provide timely, actionable data?
- Are teachers required to use multiple platforms to manage instruction and assessment?
- How easy is it to ensure assessments are aligned across grades or schools?
- What challenges exist around accessibility or accommodations for students?
