## THE THREE VALUE PILLARS

### PILLAR 1: PLAN (with precision)
Enhance planning & instruction through AI capabilities + ready-to-use content

**Table-stakes:**
- Alignment to textbooks (published programs)
- Alignment to state standards
- Expert-created item banks for core subjects (ELA, Math, Science)
- Search and discovery
- Content policy and moderation

**Delightful differentiators:**
- Alignment to ANY curriculum (white-glove customization)
- AI throughout complete loop (create → deliver → accommodate → assess → grade → analyze)
- Bring your own assets
- **NEW BTS 26:** CTE and AI Literacy offerings

**ICP Signals:**
- Recent curriculum adoption (1-3 years)
- Upcoming curriculum adoption
- In-house authored or heavily modified curricula
- Expansive tech stack relying on skills vs. standards
- Recent AI initiative (slow teacher adoption)
- Lacking supplemental resources
- Fear of "wild west" supplementing
- Recent staff cuts (low TOSA bandwidth)

---

### PILLAR 2: TEACH (with equity and engagement)
Meaningful engagement where every student can experience real success

**Table-stakes:**
- Instant feedback keeps students/teachers on track
- Broad use cases = consistency + healthy habits
- Lesson delivery paired with assessment
- Question types allowing assessment as learning
- Adjustable reading levels

**Delightful differentiators:**
- Students love the gamified experience
- Assign 25+ modifications/accommodations (unique per student)
- Set accommodations once → auto-apply going forward

**ICP Signals:**
- Recent or ongoing compliance issue
- Persistence initiatives (graduation rates)
- Dry curricula lacking engagement
- Tech stack stuffed with subject-specific tools
- Competing lesson delivery systems
- Strong equity mandates
- Teacher autonomy valued vs. top-down pedagogy
- Science of Reading mandate + grade-level content push

---

### PILLAR 3: MEASURE (with success)
Spectrum of Assessment and Reporting to Uplift Instruction and Accelerate Outcomes

**Table-stakes:**
- Instant feedback maintains momentum
- Standards-based reporting (activity/student/school level)
- Consolidation (data across formats, no tool-switching)
- Secure integrations with compliance

**Delightful differentiators:**
- Rigorous item types aligned to high-stakes tests
- Students love gamified experience (drives completion)
- District-wide common assessments (collaboration without sacrificing hours)
- AI Analyze (identify gaps, intervention groups, personalized resources)
- Anti-cheating controls

**ICP Signals:**
- Recent failed CA adoption
- Stale legacy CA tool use
- Formative data spread across bloated tech stack
- Strong PLC culture and practices
- In-building TOSAs needing coaching insights

## WAYGROUND AI — "AI where it counts"

### Core Belief
Accelerate the work of great teachers and never supplant them. AI works throughout instruction-assessment cycle to support creation, grading, accommodations, and insights.

### Framework: Create & Adapt → Assess & Guide → Analyze & Act

**Create & Digitize:**
- Instantly generate resources from scratch or digitize existing

**Adapt for Equity:**
- Differentiation: Auto-generate versions at different reading levels
- Translation: Instant ELL/ESL support
- Modifications: Reduce options for processing challenges

**Adapt for Engagement:**
- Real-world scenarios: Transform abstract to relatable
- Modify: Convert question types, add similar questions

**Assess & Guide:**
- Automated evaluation: AI grading for open-ended responses
- Standardized rubrics
- Instant hints and AI-generated explanations

**Analyze & Act:**
- Targeted grouping: AI identifies intervention groups
- AI recommendations for follow-up resources
- Reporting layers (coming soon):
  - Teacher to Admin: AI summarizes classroom data
  - Common Assessments: School-wide trends and gaps
