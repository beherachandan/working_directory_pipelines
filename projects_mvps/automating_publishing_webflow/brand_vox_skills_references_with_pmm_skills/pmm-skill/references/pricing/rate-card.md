## 6-Week Pilot Program

- Cost: $950 flat fee
- Includes: PD conducted by CSM, demonstrates value of S&D upgrade
- Credit: Full $950 credited back on full purchase
- Use case: De-risk the purchase decision; build usage data for budget justification
- Sales tip: Especially effective for "no budget right now" — locks in the relationship and creates internal champions

---

## Discount Stacking Rules

Discounts stack in this order:
1. **Volume discount** (based on student count)
2. **Bundle discount** (10% WG+CA or 15% All-In) — applied to combined price
3. **Duration discount** (5% for 2yr, 10% for 3yr) — applied after volume
4. **Additional discounts** (sales discretionary up to 5%, research partner 5%) — applied last

**Hard constraint:** Final per-student price can NEVER go below $3.00 (price floor).

---

## Key Pricing Notes for Deal Structuring

1. **School max of $9,999** means single-school deals cap out — larger deals need district-level framing
2. **School min of $1,850** means very small schools still have a minimum commitment
3. **Pilot → Purchase credit** is a powerful wedge: $950 credited back removes risk
4. **PD is included** — this is a competitive advantage. Competitors often charge separately
5. **Volume curve rewards consolidation** — every school added to a district deal drives per-student price down
6. **3-year + All-In + volume** is the maximum discount stack for large districts
7. **Research partner discount** is unique leverage for districts with strong data practices
