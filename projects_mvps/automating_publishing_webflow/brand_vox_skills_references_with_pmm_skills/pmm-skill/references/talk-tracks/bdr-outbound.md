# Talk Track: BDR Outbound

## When to Use
Cold and warm outreach to prospects. BDRs use data signals to personalize outreach and book meetings for AEs.

## Data Signals to Reference
Before reaching out, pull these from BigQuery:
- **Active teacher count** — how many teachers are already using free version
- **Games played** — volume of classroom activity
- **Paywall hits** — teachers hitting premium feature limits (strongest signal)
- **Growth trend** — is teacher adoption accelerating?
- **PQA status** — 5+ active teachers = Pre-Qualified Adopter

## Cold Outreach (No Usage Data)

### Email Template: Board Priority Hook
> Subject: [District Name]'s [board priority] initiative + Wayground
>
> Hi [Name],
>
> I noticed [District Name]'s board recently discussed [specific topic from board meeting data]. Many districts tackling [topic] are using Wayground to [relevant value prop].
>
> Would it make sense to show you how [similar district] is using it for [specific use case]?
>
> [BDR Name]

### Email Template: Peer District Reference
> Subject: How [Peer District] is using Wayground for [use case]
>
> Hi [Name],
>
> [Peer District] rolled out Wayground to support [formative assessment / engagement / etc.] and saw [specific outcome]. Given [District Name]'s focus on [priority], thought this might be relevant.
>
> Worth a 15-minute conversation?

## Warm Outreach (Usage Data Exists)

### Email Template: Teacher Activity Signal
> Subject: [X] teachers at [District Name] are already using Wayground
>
> Hi [Name],
>
> Did you know [X] teachers across [District Name] are actively using Wayground in their classrooms? They've run [Y] learning activities reaching [Z] students this [semester/quarter].
>
> Several teachers have been hitting our premium feature limits — specifically [top paywall features]. Districts like yours typically see [outcome] when they upgrade to the full platform.
>
> Would it make sense to show you what your teachers are already doing and what they could unlock?

### Email Template: Paywall Signal (Strongest)
> Subject: Your teachers are asking for more from Wayground
>
> Hi [Name],
>
> [X] teachers at [District/School] have tried to access premium features [Y] times this month. That's a strong signal they've found value in the platform and want to do more.
>
> The most-requested features are [specific features from paywall data]. I'd love to show you what unlocking these would mean for your [assessment strategy / student engagement / etc.].
>
> 15 minutes this week?

## Phone Script: PQA Account
> "Hi [Name], this is [BDR] from Wayground. I'm reaching out because [X] teachers at [District/School] are already using our platform — they've run over [Y] classroom activities this semester. A few of them have been bumping up against our free plan limits, and I wanted to see if it makes sense to show you what the full platform could do for [District Name]. Do you have 15 minutes this week?"

## Voicemail Script
> "Hi [Name], [BDR] from Wayground. Quick call — [X] of your teachers are already using our platform and several have requested premium features. I'd love to show you what they're doing and what they could unlock. I'll send a quick email with the details. Talk soon."

## Key Principles
1. **Always lead with their data** — teachers using, games played, students reached
2. **Paywall hits = highest priority signal** — teachers are literally asking for paid features
3. **Board meeting context personalizes cold outreach** — shows you did homework
4. **PQA accounts get priority** — 5+ active teachers = highest conversion probability
5. **Never sell the product in outreach** — sell the meeting
