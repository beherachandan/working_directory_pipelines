## How This Brief Works
Waygent fills Sections 1–3 from discovery data. Demo specialists use Sections 4–6 to run the call.
- **Section 1** — Account snapshot: who you're meeting, what they care about, what the stakes are.
- **Section 2** — Pillar & angle: which messaging pillar leads and the opening script.
- **Section 3** — Discovery findings: the signals Waygent surfaced and what they mean.
- **Section 4** — Demo flow: ordered steps with what to show, what to say, and why it lands.
- **Section 5** — Objection prep: 3 likely objections with Acknowledge → Reframe → Bridge responses.
- **Section 6** — Proof points: the 2-3 quotes or stats most relevant to this account.
- **Section 7** — Next step script: how to close the call with a concrete ask.

**Rule:** If the brief wasn't filled in, ask for it before the demo — not after.
