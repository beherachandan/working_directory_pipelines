# Battlecard: Edugence
*March 2026 • Confidential • Source: CA Competitive Landscape*

## Competitor Snapshot
- **Company:** Edugence (smaller player)
- **Products:** Assessment + data analytics, state reporting
- **Audience:** K-12 districts — limited brand recognition
- **Market Share:** <1% (per CA deck survey data — not listed individually)
- **Category:** Tier 2 Assessment-Adjacent Competitor (minor)

## Internal Assessment
Edugence is a smaller assessment and data analytics player with limited brand recognition and basic UX. Not commonly encountered in competitive deals. If mentioned, it's usually a legacy installation in a smaller district.

## Core Weaknesses
- **Limited brand recognition** — Most districts haven't heard of it
- **Basic UX** — Interface feels dated
- **Small team** — Slower feature development, limited support resources
- **No engagement or gamification**
- **No AI features**
- **Limited scale** — May not support large district deployments reliably

## When You'll See Them
- Smaller districts with legacy installations
- Not a common competitive encounter — treat as opportunity for easy displacement

## Deal Strategy
- Lead with everything Wayground offers that Edugence doesn't: engagement, AI, accommodations, CA, daily use
- Should be a straightforward displacement conversation
- Don't over-research or over-prepare for this competitor — standard platform value story wins

## THE ONE-LINER
> "Wayground does what Edugence does, plus everything else teachers and students need every day."
