## How to Use This Document
This is a live decision tool — not background reading. Use it in real time to route a district conversation to the right pillar and the right proof points.
- **Waygent AI:** Use Section 1 to identify pillar, then pull matching talk tracks and demo moments into the discovery brief.
- **Sales reps:** Scan Section 2 before the call to identify the persona and confirm your angle.
- **Demo specialists:** Use the 'Best demo moment' column in Section 1 to set the demo flow.
- **Rule of thumb:** If you hear 2+ signals in the same pillar, that's your lead. If signals are mixed, default to PLAN — it's the broadest entry point.

## The Three Pillars — Quick Reference

| Pillar | Focus | Teacher Headline | Admin Headline |
|---|---|---|---|
| **PLAN** | AI content creation, curriculum alignment, resource management | "Find the right materials at the right time." | "Protect your curriculum investment." |
| **TEACH** | 25+ accommodations, gamification, differentiation, student access | "Engagement starts with access." | "Students engage when they can access the content." |
| **MEASURE** | Formative assessment, real-time data, Common Assessments, reporting | "Get data that reflects what students know." | "Formative data you can trust." |

## SECTION 2: Persona-to-Pillar Routing Guide

| Persona | What Keeps Them Up | Their Language | Lead Value Prop | Don't Say |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Teacher** | Not enough time. Students zoning out. Too many platforms. | "I spend my weekends..." / "Same 5 students answer" / "Which app?" | Time savings + engagement that lasts | "This will save you time" (show, don't tell) |
| **Dir. of Curriculum** | Teachers going off-curriculum. Can't see if curriculum works. | "Curriculum fidelity" / "Supplemental alignment" / "PLCs need data" | Protect the curriculum investment | "Save teacher time" (wrong priority) |
| **IT Director** | 2,700+ tools, security nightmares, low adoption. | "Tech stack" / "SSO" / "Adoption rates" / "COPPA/FERPA" | Consolidation play | Curriculum/pedagogical language (not their lens) |
| **Principal** | State test scores, teacher burnout, proving ROI. | "Achievement gaps" / "Retention" / "Board presentation" | Outcomes + visibility | Feature lists or technical details |
| **Tech Coach** | Supporting 120+ teachers across 7 platforms. | "I spend all my time troubleshooting" / "Adoption stalls" | One platform to go deep on | "This is easy to learn" (they need depth) |
